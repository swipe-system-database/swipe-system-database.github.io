Show Overview

Summary

Swipe System is a comic series created by Terraversal. Each chapter has its own unique story and adventure, generally following the storyline of Bob and Sophia.

Basic Information

  • Genre: Adventure, comedy, surreal.
  • Created By: Terraversal, Valkman, All Peace Above.
  • Developed By: Terraversal Comics.
  • Original Network/Platform: terraversalcomics.univer.se.
  • Release Date: Approximately September, 25, 2025.
  • Status: Ongoing.
  • Number of Volumes: 5+.
  • Target Audience: All ages, some scenes may be intense for children under the age of 5.
